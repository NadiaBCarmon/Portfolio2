The Art of Open-Ended Horror: An Interview with Indie Author James D Lopez

LA-based indie author James D. Lopez is a creator of bite-sized horror fiction & picture books for adults. His debut anthology Unspeakable is a collection of short stories that are in length and tone similar to H.P. Lovecraft's fast fiction. With the material for a second book in progress, he talks about his inspirations; which includes Tolkien, Ambrose Bierce, Dr. Seuss, and more.

We're always trying to get things right through art that we couldn't in life.

Keith Walker, Filmmaker
